User Guideline

1. Sentiment Analyzer

Input the sentiment text relevant to the socioeconomic theme in the designated field. Click the "Analyze" button to classify the sentiment.

2. Understanding the classification

  • Positive sentiment: Indicates favorable or optimistic emotions. Statements show happiness, success, approval.
  • Negative sentiment: Unfavorable or pessimistic emotions, conveys dissatisfaction, frustration, criticism.
  • Neutral sentiment: Expresses neither strong positivity nor negativity; statements are factual, balanced, or lack emotional bias.
