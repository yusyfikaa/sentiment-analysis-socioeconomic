SOCIOECONOMIC AND SOCIAL MEDIA

Socioeconomics studies how economic and social factors impact individuals and society. Social media amplifies these effects by shaping public opinion, influencing behavior, and providing platforms for discussions on socioeconomic issues, allowing real-time sentiment analysis and insights into societal trends and concerns.

SENTIMENT ANALYSIS

Sentiment analysis refers to the use of NLP and machine learning to identify and extract subjective information from text. It evaluates whether sentiment is positive, negative, or neutral; it is often used to analyze social media, reviews, and customer feedback as a way to gauge public opinion or emotional response.

Overview:

This system analyzes social media posts to detect sentiments related to socioeconomic impacts like public health, inflation, cost of living, and employment trends.
